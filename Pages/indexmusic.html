    About Me:

    I'm Mexican, and live in Cuernavaca, Morelos, and I was born here too. I studied Computer Systems Engineering & Contemporary Music.

    I have extensive experience directing string ensembles, choral groups, fusion projects; transcribing, teaching, arranging, and performing fusion jazz, progressive metal, and contemporary music.

    This work has allowed me to gain recognition in Morelos and collaborate on educational and research projects, thanks to my passion for composition, music theory, leadership, technology, and various methodologies that allow my work to unfold between functionality, purpose, and above all, art.

    As a Composer, I have a passion for crafting stunning and engaging experiences in the musical realm. With a strong background in art principles and a keen eye for aesthetics, I specialize progressive & contemporary music, captivating technical, and innovative aspects between many musical strategies. I thrive on staying up-to-date with the latest design trends and technologies to deliver cutting-edge solutions that not only meet client objectives but also leave a lasting impact on every student.
