Some Introduction:

The Morelos State Science Museum, established in 2012, is a beacon of scientific knowledge and exploration located in Cuernavaca, Morelos. Its mission is to make science accessible to all and promote scientific curiosity throughout the state. This dynamic museum offers a vast array of exhibits covering various scientific disciplines, from physics and chemistry to biology and astronomy. Whether you're a budding scientist, a curious student, or someone seeking to satisfy your thirst for knowledge, you'll find something to ignite your curiosity within its walls. The Morelos State Science Museum is deeply committed to bringing science education to the people of Morelos. It organizes outreach programs, workshops, and interactive exhibits in communities across the state. Mobile science units travel to schools, fairs, and events, ensuring that science reaches those who may not have the opportunity to visit the museum in person. In addition to its physical presence, the museum embraces the digital age, providing online resources, virtual exhibits, and interactive webinars, making science education accessible to all, regardless of location. The museum takes inclusivity seriously, offering special programs and accommodations for visitors with disabilities to ensure that everyone can partake in the joy of scientific exploration. Throughout the years, the Morelos State Science Museum has witnessed countless moments of awe and inspiration as visitors of all ages engage with its exhibits. Children and adults alike are captivated by the hands-on displays, immersing themselves in the wonder of scientific discovery.
